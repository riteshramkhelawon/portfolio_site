RITESH RAMKHELAWON

London, UK
Developer

I am a full stack developer with experience in Java, mainly Groovy. I have experience in using the Grails framework to develop web applications. In my current post, I am responsible for the architecture of the software we are building, as well as developing and maintaining features.

I am a team player and love the opportunity to learn from the experience of more senior developers, as well as sharing my knowledge to more junior team members. I take pride in my attention to detail and project planning skills, and have used these to great length whilst developing an online marketplace web application from the ground up, within 3 months.
